About Me

Info

How I became interested in research.

The ability for people to communicate with each other is something that I value greatly.

I remember a moment in grade school when I found some children playing. I wanted to join in but I could not understand their words. They couldn't understand mine.

As children often did, and do. We innovated. We pointed, we gestured, and laughed. We had fun.

It is this freedom and desire for open and succesful communication that I hope to disseminate and maintain in all of my endeavors.
