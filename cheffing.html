What is cheffing?

Cheffing is an artistic expression

Cheffing is an unforgiving science

You must be intimitaly aware of your ingridients' finest qualities and their most finicky temperaments.

It is your role as conductor to help them blossom and encourage their individual expression of their personalities, their talents, their passions.

And care must be taken not to play favorites. All the members you have so lovingly selected and cared after must get along and play nice with each other.

No showboats allowed or you risk loosing the balance that is being so delicately crafted.

All of this while in the midst of battling angry destructive flames who laughingly threaten to destroy your friends who helplessly look up at you through innocent misty eyes.

You put them in the situation they are now in, which makes you solely responsible for their wellbeing.

With fevereshness and compassion you tumble them to and fro, protecting them, bathing them, loving them.

You boldly push their limits, you are taking a risk, but you know they cannot bloom if they stay bound within fear. Nor you.

That's it. Enough! It wasn't the timer, or a little voice. It was you. A feeling, a shadow, a touch. You know they are almost there.

Swiftly you remove them from the flame. You stand back and watch them rest.

You dare not disturb them. This is their time. They have earned it.

Quietly and ceremoniously you prepare a canvas for them. Only the best will do for their hard work.

Now you present them to your guests.

And like the conductor of a great symphony who with eyes closed listens to the last note as it makes it's final and dramatic solo exit, you feel pride.

Both you and the conductor know that the pride you feel is not pride in yourselves, but pride in the members that blosssomed and shone.

The applause at the concert hall and the ohhs and ahhs at the dining table are for them.

And when it is all done. What you have is beauty, pleasure, love.

Cheffing is seduction.
