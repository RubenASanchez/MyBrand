PDF Journal Article Annotation Software:

A review of ColWiz, Mendeley, Qiqqa, and Zotero.


Publish Date

Author

November 15, 2015

Ruben A Sanchez

In this article I review 2 popular journal annotators and as a bonus I uncluded 2 which you may not be aware of. I have organized the review into 4 sections beginning with a short description of what journal article annotators generally do, then I give a bird's eye view description conentrating on the major advantages and disadvantages of each, following is a more robust comparison, and the last section presents the key points of the review in a summary table. Finally, each receives a score between 0 and 5. Of course, this is my opinion, but I do hope that you find this review helpful as you decide which is the best journal annotator and organizer for you.
