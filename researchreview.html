Introduction


In this article I review 2 popular journal annotators and as a bonus I included 2 others which you may not have heard of. I have organized the review into 4 sections beginning with a short description of what journal article annotators generally do, then I give a bird's eye view describing the major advantages and disadvantages of each, next I give a more robust comparison, and in the last section I present the key points of the review in a summary table. Finally, each annotator/organizer receives a score between 0 and 5. I do hope that you find this review helpful as you decide which is the best journal annotator and organizer for your use.
